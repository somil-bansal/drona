Trip to Amsterdam

June 9th - 16th | Budget: ~$7500 total

Itinerary & Attractions

Explore the best of Amsterdam with a mix of cultural and scenic attractions:

  • Rijksmuseum: Admire Dutch art and history.
  • Anne Frank House: Experience a moving piece of history.
  • Van Gogh Museum: Discover the masterpieces of Vincent van Gogh.
  • Dam Square: Visit the historic heart of the city.
  • Vondelpark: Enjoy a relaxing day in an urban park.
  • Canal Cruise: View the city from a different perspective on its famous waterways.

Additional free time can be set aside to wander the charming streets, explore local cafés, and soak up Amsterdam's unique culture.

Basic Dutch Phrases

Helpful Dutch words and phrases to assist you in your travels:

  • Hallo: Hello
  • Goedemorgen: Good morning
  • Dank je wel: Thank you
  • Alsjeblieft: Please/You're welcome
  • Waar is …? Where is …?
  • Tot ziens: Goodbye

Flight Options

Round-trip flight options for your journey:

  • Several reputable airlines offer competitive rates to Amsterdam Schiphol Airport.
  • Book early to secure prices in the range of $600-$800 per person depending on departure location and promotions.
  • Consider package deals available from travel aggregators to optimize your budget.

Hotel Options

Comfortable accommodations in central Amsterdam that fit within the budget:

  • Hotel Estherea: A boutique-style hotel with easy access to attractions.
  • NH Collection Amsterdam Grand Hotel: Offers both luxury and convenience.
  • Consider other mid-range hotels that balance cost and comfort to manage staying within a $7500 budget when combined with flight costs.

Budget Considerations

Keep your trip within a ~$7500 budget by:

  • Comparing flight prices and booking early or during promotions.
  • Reserving hotels in advance and considering packages that include amenities such as breakfast.
  • Planning daily itineraries with many free or low-cost attractions and activities.

Enjoy your trip to Amsterdam! Safe travels!
